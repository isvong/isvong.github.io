about

Hello! My name is Isabella Vong, a 4th year student at the University of Michigan pursuing a B.S. Computer Science & Ethnic Studies. I work as a research assistant under Matthew Bui in the Race and Data Justice Lab. As a researcher, I am interested in the use of technology in urban spaces, especially with regard to racial minorities and ethnic enclaves living in these places. As a programmer, I am interested in art and game development.
