about

Hello! My name is Isabella Vong, a 4th year student at the University of Michigan pursuing an undergraduate degree in Computer Science & Ethnic Studies. I work as a research assistant under Dr. Matthew Bui in the Race and Data Justice Lab. As a researcher. I am also advised by Dr. Melissa Borja in the current development of my Honors Thesis. I am interested in the ways which technology affect communities of color, ranging from developments in urban ethnic enclaves to the most rural ethnoburbs. Outside of my academic research, I am also interested in art and game development as a hobby! :)
