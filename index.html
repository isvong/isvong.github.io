about

my name is Isabella Vong (she/they) a 3rd year undergraduate at the university of michigan where i double major in computer science and prospectively american culture/ethnic studies alongside a minor in digital studies. i am interested in the intersection of race, society, and technology.

i am curious about the staggered experiences in contemporary computing, specifically in the marginalized and/or displaced. i also have a special interest in social movements and the modern role of technology in relation to greater social efforts.

please reach out to me if you are interested in further discussing any of the topics above! :)
