about

my name is isabella (she/they), a 3rd year undergraduate at the university of michigan studying computer science with a minor in digital studies.

interests

my current focus lies in the histories and methods of "non-mainstream" and/or marginalized groups online

i am intrigued by:

  • the obfuscation and neglect of these groups / the overcoming of these barriers in making these groups visible
  • the dimensions and formations of "web culture," specifically of the ideals of a democratized and open Internet, created through communities and user effort.
  • the complexities in the use of technology for social good

i like to explore these topics through:

  • interactive storytelling
  • longform journalism/formal blogging
  • formal research and the development of tools (applications/programs)

i would like to think of myself as an advocate for thought and creativity. i wholeheartedly believe the internet should be a free and open resource in which everyone is able to access information.
